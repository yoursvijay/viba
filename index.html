Viba Employees

An all-in-one employee management system for complete regulation of employee workflow. Viba employee management liberates HR teams and employees from ledgers and slower-than-sloth biometrics.

  • Touchless clock-in, clock-out from the office/Viba Pod or anywhere from the world.
  • Mask and temperature detection, and facial recognition.
  • Pre-invitation feature to invite visitors
  • Report generation to manage payroll, attendance, leave management, and more.

Viba Visitors

Viba maintains a track and guides visitors at workplaces, keeping all parties in loop.Viba alerts HR and employees about the following in time to allow for a seamless experience.

  • 100% touchless check-in & check-out experience
  • Temperature & Mask detection
  • Quick Check-in via Pre-invitation/QR Code.
  • Get the visitor badge at the time of Visitor check-in.

Viba Meeting Rooms

Making the meeting experience easier than ever. Viba meetings allows employees to view and book available meeting rooms, invite attendees and chat, without having to hop between other apps.

  • Employees can book meetings and invite attendees including outside organization users.
  • Quick check-in by the visitor at the time of check-in.
  • Easy sync with office365 and google calendars.
  • View available meeting rooms.

Push Notifications

Leave Management

Viba Pod

Viba Pod is the gateway to a winning workplace.

Step One

100% Touchless

Viba Pod provides a 100% touchless clock-in and clock-out experience to employees and visitors.

Step Two

Facial detection and recognition

Viba can detect, recognize and distinguish between old and new faces.

Step Three

Mask Detection

Viba detects a lack of masks on the face and instructs visitors or employees to wear them. This feature can be customized via the admin app

Step Four

Hand Sanitisation

Visitors and employees can sanitize their hands at any time

Step Five

SOS alert

The admin team can send an SOS alert in case of an emergency
