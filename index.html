How Viba
Works?

VIBA works like a Magic Wand for enabling Workplace transformation with AI

VIBA is the most comprehensive solution for businesses whose aim is Workplace Transformation using AI. VIBA is a cloud based touchless attendance management system. The solution is designed to bring transparency & control in the Attendance process and also visitor tracking. By automating tedious attendance workflows and tasks, managers and admins can gain visibility and increase efficiency in the organisation. VIBA alleviates the burden on the management team, allowing them to save precious time on checking attendance.
